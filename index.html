Hugo del Valle-Inclán

Faculty of Economics · Office 335 · (+34) 986 813 530 · hvalle-inclan [at] uvigo [dot] es

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Vigo, and a member of ECOBAS and EQUALITAS. In the Spring 2019 I was a visiting scholar at the department of economics at Johns Hopkins University.

My research interests include economic inequality, the theories of distributive justice, data analysis and coding. Currently I am working on the measurement of opportunity inequality.

One of my working papers won the Aldi Hagenaars Memorial Award.

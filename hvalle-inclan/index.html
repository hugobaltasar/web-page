Hugo del Valle-Inclán Cruces

Faculty of Economics · Office 335 · Campus Lagoas-Marcosende 36310 · (+34) 986 813 530 · hvalle-inclan [at] uvigo.es

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Vigo under the supervision of Carlos Hervés-Beloso, and a member of RGEA (Research Group in Economic Analysis) and ECOBAS (Economics and Business Administration for Society).

My research interests lie in the theories of distributive justice, data analysis and coding. Currently I am working on the measurement of (in)equality of opportunity.
